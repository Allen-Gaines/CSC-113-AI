## Key Learnings

### 1. Prompt Interpretation Varies
Different AI tools interpret the same prompt differently. ChatGPT focused on "space" and "defense" but missed the "tower" aspect entirely.

### 2. Iteration is Essential
The initial Claude prototype was basic. Through 10+ iterations, we added:
- Multiple turret types
- Enemy variety
- Upgrade system
- Special abilities
- Visual polish

### 3. AI Tool Selection Matters
For game development, Claude demonstrated better understanding of game mechanics and design patterns.

### 4. Specificity Helps
More detailed prompts yield better results. Instead of "tower defense game," specifying "click to place stationary turrets that auto-fire at enemies walking along a path" would be clearer.

## Recommendations for Future AI-Assisted Development

1. **Be Specific**: Include exact mechanics in prompts
2. **Iterate**: First output is rarely final
3. **Compare Tools**: Different AIs have different strengths
4. **Test Early**: Catch misunderstandings quickly
5. **Document Changes**: Track what worked and what didn't
